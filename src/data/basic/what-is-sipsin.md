# 십성이란 무엇인가?

십성(十星)은 사주를 해석하는 핵심 도구 중 하나라네.

일간을 기준으로 다른 글자와의 관계를 열 가지 유형으로 나눈 것이지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
# What is Sipsin (The Ten Gods)?

Sipsin (十星), often translated as the Ten Gods, is one of the most important tools used in Saju interpretation.

It categorizes the relationships between the Day Master (Ilgan) and the other elements in the chart into ten distinct patterns.

## The Ten Types of Sipsin

The Ten Gods consist of the following categories:

* Bigyeon (Companion)
* Geopjae (Competitor)
* Siksin (Eating God)
* Sanggwan (Hurting Officer)
* Pyeonjae (Indirect Wealth)
* Jeongjae (Direct Wealth)
* Pyeongwan (Seven Killings)
* Jeonggwan (Direct Officer)
* Pyeonin (Indirect Resource)
* Jeongin (Direct Resource)
<!-- /en -->

## 십성은 무엇을 의미하나?

십성은 사람의 행동 방식과 관계를 보여준다네.

예를 들면

* 비견 → 독립성
* 식신 → 표현력
* 정재 → 현실 감각
* 정관 → 책임감

과 같은 의미를 가진다네.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## What Do the Ten Gods Represent?

The Ten Gods reveal how a person interacts with the world and responds to different situations.

For example:

* Bigyeon (Companion) → Independence and self-reliance
* Siksin (Eating God) → Creativity and self-expression
* Jeongjae (Direct Wealth) → Practicality and financial awareness
* Jeonggwan (Direct Officer) → Responsibility and discipline

Each God reflects a different aspect of personality, motivation, and behavior.
<!-- /en -->

## 십성이 중요한 이유

사주에 같은 오행이 있어도 십성 구조에 따라 성격과 삶의 방향이 크게 달라질 수 있다네.

그래서 실전 명리학에서는 십성을 매우 중요하게 본다네.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## Why Are the Ten Gods Important?

Even when two people share similar Five Element balances, their personalities and life paths can differ dramatically depending on the structure of their Ten Gods.

For this reason, professional Saju practitioners consider Sipsin one of the most powerful tools for understanding character, relationships, career tendencies, and life direction.
