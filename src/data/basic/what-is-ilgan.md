# What is Ilgan (Day Master)?

If you are new to Saju, the very first concept you should learn is the Ilgan.

Ilgan (日干), often translated as the Day Master, is the Heavenly Stem of your birth day and represents your true self within the Saju chart.
<!-- /en -->

---

<!-- ko -->
## 왜 일간이 중요한가?

모든 사주 해석은 일간을 기준으로 시작한다네.

일간이 무엇인지 알아야

* 십성
* 용신
* 대운

등을 제대로 해석할 수 있지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## Why is the Day Master Important?

Every Saju interpretation begins with the Day Master.

Without understanding your Ilgan, it becomes difficult to accurately interpret concepts such as:

* Ten Gods (Sipsin)
* Yongshin (Useful Element)
* Daewoon (Major Luck Cycle)

The Day Master serves as the reference point for the entire chart.

## The Ten Day Masters

There are ten possible Day Masters in Saju:

* Gap Wood (甲木)
* Eul Wood (乙木)
* Byeong Fire (丙火)
* Jeong Fire (丁火)
* Mu Earth (戊土)
* Gi Earth (己土)
* Gyeong Metal (庚金)
* Sin Metal (辛金)
* Im Water (壬水)
* Gye Water (癸水)
<!-- /en -->

<!-- en -->
## Characteristics of Each Day Master

Each Day Master is often compared to a natural image.

For example:

* Gap Wood (甲木) is like a large tree.
* Jeong Fire (丁火) is like a candle flame.
* Gyeong Metal (庚金) is like raw steel.
* Im Water (壬水) is like a mighty river.

Because each element expresses itself differently, every Day Master has its own way of thinking, acting, and responding to life.

## What Can Be Learned from the Day Master Alone?

Even knowing only your Day Master can reveal valuable insights about:

* Core personality traits
* Natural strengths
* Potential weaknesses
* Relationship tendencies

While a full Saju analysis requires examining the entire chart, the Day Master alone already provides a meaningful understanding of one's character.
