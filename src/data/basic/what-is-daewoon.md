# 대운이란 무엇인가?

사주가 타고난 설계도라면 대운은 인생의 계절이라네.

대운(大運)은 약 10년 단위로 바뀌는 큰 운의 흐름을 의미하지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
# What is Daewoon (Major Luck Cycle)?

If Saju is the blueprint you are born with, Daewoon is the changing seasons of your life.

Daewoon (大運), often translated as the Major Luck Cycle, refers to the large flow of fortune that changes approximately every ten years.
<!-- /en -->

---

<!-- ko -->
## 왜 같은 사주인데 삶이 달라질까?

사주가 같더라도 살아가는 시기에 따라 운의 흐름이 달라진다네.

그 이유가 바로 대운 때문이지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## Why Do People with the Same Saju Live Different Lives?

Even when two people share the same Saju chart, the timing of their lives can unfold very differently.

The primary reason is the influence of Daewoon, which changes the overall environment and opportunities surrounding a person.
<!-- /en -->

---

<!-- ko -->
## 대운의 특징

대운은 보통 10년 단위로 변화한다네.

예를 들면

* 20세~29세
* 30세~39세
* 40세~49세

와 같은 흐름으로 흘러가지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## Characteristics of Daewoon

A Daewoon cycle generally changes every ten years.

For example:

* Ages 20–29
* Ages 30–39
* Ages 40–49

Each period carries a different energetic influence and life theme.
<!-- /en -->

---

<!-- ko -->
## 좋은 대운이란?

좋은 대운은 용신을 도와주는 대운이라네.

반대로 기신이 강해지는 시기에는 어려움을 겪기도 하지.
<!-- /ko -->
<!-- en -->
## What Makes a Good Daewoon?

A favorable Daewoon is one that supports and strengthens your Yongshin (Useful Element).

On the other hand, when an unfavorable element becomes dominant, life may feel more challenging or demanding.
<!-- /en -->

* 사업

등의 큰 흐름을 판단할 때 활용한다네.

## What Can Daewoon Reveal?

Daewoon is commonly used to analyze major life trends such as:

* Career changes
* Business opportunities
* Marriage and relationships
* Wealth and finances
* Health and well-being

Rather than predicting specific events, it helps identify broader life patterns and timing.
